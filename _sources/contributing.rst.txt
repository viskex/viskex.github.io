How to contribute
=================

        viskex developement takes place on GitHub. We are still at an early developement stage.

| Developement takes place on our `GitHub repository <https://github.com/viskex/viskex>`__.
| **viskex** developement is still at very early stages.
| Contributions improving either the code or the documentation, as well as proposal of new features, are welcome as `pull requests <https://github.com/viskex/viskex/pulls>`__.
| Bug reports and upcoming feature proposals can be reported to `our issue tracker <https://github.com/viskex/viskex/issues>`__.

For further information or questions about **viskex** feel free to contact us by `email <mailto:francesco.ballarin@unicatt.it>`__ as well.
